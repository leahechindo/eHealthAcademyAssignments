Spicy Spaghetti

Ingredients Recipe Details Steps
  • Spicy Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Spaghetti
  • Seasoning
  • Pre-fried chicken chunks
  • Groundnut oil
  • Green and Yellow bell peppers
  • 1 cup of spicy tomato sauce
  • 1 bulb of onions
  • 1 packet of spaghetti (preferably tiny strands)
  • 1 and a half tablespoons of seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of groundnut oil
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper

Steam the spaghetti to a slightly soft texture,
Drain out water and rinse through with cold water to stop steaming.
Put a clean frying pan on the fire and allow to dry properly the add oil.
Allow oil to simmer for 1 minute then add chopped onions and the spicy sauce.
Allow to simmer for 2-3 minutes then add chopped bell peppers and seasoning.
After 1 minutes, add spaghetti and pre-fried chicken.
Allow to simmer for 5 minutes and serve.

Chicken and Chips

Ingredients Recipe Details Steps
  • chicken
  • ground black pepper
  • sea salt
  • dried rosemary
  • chilli powder (optional)
  • irish potatoes
  • sunflower or vegetable oil
  • 6 pieces of chicken
  • 100ml olive oil
  • 2tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1tsp sea salt
  • 2tsp dried rosemary
  • 1tsp chilli powder (optional)
  • 5-6 irish potatoes
  • 1 litre sunflower or vegetable oil

For the chicken, put all the ingredients (including the chilli, if using) in a freezer bag and massage the chicken all over with the oil, pepper, salt and rosemary.
Refrigerate for 2 hrs or overnight.
Preheat the oven to 200°C/400°F/Gas Mark 4.
Remove the chicken from the freezer bag and transfer to a roasting tray.
Cook in the oven for 40-45 mins, until the juices run clear when you insert a skewer into the thickest part of the meat.
Meanwhile, to make the fries,
Peel the potatoes and slice into matchstick-sized piece.
Add the oil to a large frying pan, wok or deep fat fryer then cook on a medium heat for around 15 mins until the fries become crisp and golden.
Drain on kitchen paper and serve with a piece of chicken.

Can be served with mayonnaise or ketchup
